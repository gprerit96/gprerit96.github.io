PRERIT GUPTA

Deep Learning Enthusiast & Analyst

Born and brought up in Noida, which is a city close to the national capital of India - Delhi, my interest centers around building intelligent systems, analyse data and perform learning of complex tasks. Being a nature lover, I percieve Engineering as a means to worship the divine creations of nature. My curiosity lies to understand and design artificial systems by harnessing the untapped potential of natural creations. I like working in areas which involve creativity, out of the box thinking and a penchant to unveil hidden mysteries.

Currently, I am a final year student in the Department of Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering and am working with Prof. Sudipta Mukhopadhyay on generating synthetic time series data for Behavioral Biometrics with Generative Adversarial Networks (GAN). My research interests are Machine Learning, Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision.
